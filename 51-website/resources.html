Ocaml

For the first two thirds of the class, we will be using Objective Caml (or Ocaml for short) to do most of our programming assignments.

We would recommend that you install Ocaml on your own computers. It is also installed on nice.fas.harvard.edu.

The book:

Developing Applications with Objective Caml, by E.Chailloux, P.Manoury, and B.Pagano
